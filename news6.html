Call of Duty 2025 Announced

Published on: July 30, 2025

Next-Gen COD Returns to Modern Warfare Roots

Activision has confirmed that the next installment in the Call of Duty franchise will be released in November 2025. The game, developed by Infinity Ward, will reportedly return to modern-day combat with a global storyline and revamped multiplayer mechanics.

Sources indicate that Call of Duty 2025 will feature real-time weather effects, new movement mechanics, and an updated Warzone integration. A beta test is expected later this year.

Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, PC
Developer: Infinity Ward
Genre: First-Person Shooter, Action

Highlights:

  • Campaign with globe-trotting missions
  • New engine enhancements
  • Co-op and Zombies returning
  • Crossplay and anti-cheat improvements
