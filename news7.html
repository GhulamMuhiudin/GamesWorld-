eSports Viewership Breaks Records

Published on: July 29, 2025

Massive Surge in Global eSports Audience in 2025

The eSports industry continues its meteoric rise as global viewership reaches record-breaking levels in 2025. Recent tournaments such as League of Legends Worlds, The International, and Valorant Champions have attracted tens of millions of concurrent viewers.

According to industry analytics, total global eSports viewership is projected to surpass 750 million by the end of this year. This explosive growth is being fueled by increased accessibility, mobile streaming platforms, and younger audiences embracing competitive gaming as mainstream entertainment.

Major Milestones: The 2025 League of Legends World Championship peaked at over 12 million live concurrent viewers, setting a new benchmark for any eSports event in history. Meanwhile, regional events in Asia and Europe are seeing unprecedented engagement and sponsor interest.

With record sponsorship deals, rising prize pools, and growing inclusion in traditional sports media, experts predict that eSports will soon rival traditional sports like football and basketball in global influence.
