Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree

Published on: July 30, 2025

Massive Expansion Revealed for Elden Ring Fans

FromSoftware has officially announced Shadow of the Erdtree, a massive DLC expansion for the critically acclaimed game Elden Ring. This expansion promises new lore, massive new regions, terrifying bosses, and complex dungeons.

The reveal trailer has already captivated fans with glimpses of mysterious golden fields and long-forgotten ruins. According to insiders, this will be FromSoftware's most ambitious expansion yet.

Key Features:

  • New areas larger than Limgrave
  • 10+ new bosses
  • Expanded magic and weapons
  • More endings and lore revelations
