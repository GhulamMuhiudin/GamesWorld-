Xbox Acquires Bethesda

Published on: July 29, 2025

Microsoft Expands Xbox Game Studios with Major Acquisition

In a historic move, Microsoft has officially acquired Bethesda Softworks and its parent company ZeniMax Media, bringing iconic franchises like The Elder Scrolls, Fallout, Doom, and Starfield under the Xbox Game Studios banner.

The $7.5 billion acquisition marks one of the largest in gaming history, strengthening Microsoft’s position in the gaming industry. Phil Spencer, head of Xbox, stated, “With the addition of Bethesda, we are investing deeply in the future of gaming one that puts the player at the center and empowers them with more great games on Xbox, PC, and Game Pass.”

Impact: Bethesda’s upcoming titles are expected to launch day one on Game Pass, and some future titles may become Xbox exclusives. This move significantly boosts Xbox’s first-party development capabilities.

Fans are eagerly waiting to see how this will shape the future of AAA game development and exclusivity in the coming years.
