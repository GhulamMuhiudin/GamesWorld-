Cyberpunk 2077 Sequel Teased

Published on: July 29, 2025

CD Projekt Red Teases Next Chapter in Cyberpunk Universe

CD Projekt Red has officially confirmed that a **Cyberpunk 2077 sequel** is in early development, thrilling fans who have remained loyal to the dystopian franchise.

Codenamed Project Orion, the game promises to build upon the world and mechanics of Cyberpunk 2077, with deeper storytelling, enhanced AI, and a brand-new cityscape to explore. The studio has expressed its ambition to create a “true evolution” of the original, pushing boundaries with next-gen gameplay.

Status: In Development
Expected Release: TBA (Possibly 2026–2027)
Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, PC
Developer: CD Projekt Red
Genre: Action RPG, Sci-Fi, Open World

More details are expected in the coming months, as fans speculate about where the story will go after the events of Night City.
