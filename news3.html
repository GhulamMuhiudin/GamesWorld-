PS5 Pro Leaked

Published on: July 29, 2025

A sneak peek into Sony’s next-gen upgrade with 8K support and better frame rates

Rumors have intensified as new leaks suggest that Sony is preparing to launch a PS5 Pro model featuring upgraded hardware capable of supporting 8K resolution and delivering smoother frame rates across both current and upcoming PlayStation titles.

Sources close to Sony’s manufacturing partners revealed early prototype specs, pointing to enhanced ray tracing, improved cooling systems, and possibly a new design form factor. The Pro model is expected to feature a more powerful GPU and faster memory bandwidth, giving developers greater freedom to push graphical boundaries.

Expected Features:

  • 8K video output support
  • Higher frame rates (up to 120 FPS)
  • Advanced ray tracing capabilities
  • Faster SSD performance
  • Enhanced AI-based upscaling technology

Sony has yet to confirm these leaks officially, but insiders expect a formal announcement in late 2025 with a potential release date in early 2026.
