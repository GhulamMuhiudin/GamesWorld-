The Sims 5 Confirmed

Published on: July 30, 2025

Next Generation of Life Simulation Is Almost Here

EA has finally confirmed the development of The Sims 5, which will feature a completely open-world environment and integrated multiplayer functionality. Fans can now look forward to building homes and managing lives not only solo but with friends online.

In a teaser video, developers showed real-time neighborhood interactions, dynamic weather changes, and highly customizable avatars. The game will also support cross-platform play across PC and consoles.

New Features:

  • True open world navigation
  • Online multiplayer neighborhoods
  • Deep customization and AI behaviors
  • Modding tools from day one
