Game Overview

PUBG: Battlegrounds is a multiplayer battle royale shooter that pits 100 players against each other in a last-man-standing match. Developed by PUBG Studios and published by Krafton, it has become one of the most iconic and influential shooters of all time. Land on a vast map, scavenge for weapons, gear, and vehicles, and outlast opponents using your wits, aim, and survival instincts. Whether you play solo, duo, or squad every match is a thrilling challenge.

Key Features

  • > Iconic battle royale with up to 100 players
  • > Realistic weapons and ballistics system
  • > Multiple maps like Erangel, Miramar, Vikendi, and Taego
  • > Ranked play, arcade modes, and custom lobbies
  • > Frequent updates, new gear, and seasonal content
  • > Free-to-play with optional cosmetic purchases
