Game Overview

GTA 6, the very name sends waves of excitement through the gaming world. After years of speculation and hope, Rockstar Games has finally confirmed the next chapter in the Grand Theft Auto series. It’s official: GTA 6 is real, and the hype is massive. Video games have always brought people together and sparked global moments, even in this generation when mobile games have started to bloom. From lifelong fans to casual players, everyone is watching closely to see what Rockstar delivers next. It’s been over a decade since GTA 5 launched in 2013. The kids who were 10 to 13 back then are now in their 20s, still holding onto the same excitement, only now with a decade’s worth of anticipation. Grand Theft Auto isn’t just a game; it’s a generational experience created by Rockstar using their game engine, RAGE. Following years of speculation and anticipation, Rockstar confirmed in February 2022 that the game was in development. That September, footage from unfinished versions was leaked on the internet in what journalists described as one of the biggest leaks in the history of the video game industry. The game was formally revealed in December 2023 and is scheduled to be released on 26 May 2026 for the PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S.

  • > Realistic vehicle handling and customization
