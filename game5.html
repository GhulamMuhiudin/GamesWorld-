Game Overview

The Outer Worlds 2 returns with a brand-new star system, fresh characters, and a sarcastic twist on sci-fi RPGs. Developed by Obsidian Entertainment, the game promises more freedom, deeper role-playing mechanics, and the same dark humor that made the original a fan favorite. Set in a corporate-dominated galaxy, players will once again shape their story through impactful choices, branching dialogue, and dynamic combat. With upgraded visuals, new alien worlds to explore, and expanded customization for weapons and companions, The Outer Worlds 2 delivers a bold and witty journey through space where every decision can change the outcome.

Key Features

  • > All-new solar system with unexplored planets
  • > Enhanced character creation and deeper role-playing paths
  • > Larger cities and complex faction relationships
  • > Signature Obsidian humor with new satirical writing
  • > Improved companion AI and questlines
  • > Next-gen graphics engine and smooth performance
