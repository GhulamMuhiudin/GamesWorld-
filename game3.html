Game Overview

Ghost of Yōtei transports players to a mystical and war-torn feudal Japan, shadowed by the towering presence of Mount Yōtei. In this atmospheric open-world action-adventure, you step into the role of a lone samurai turned spirit warrior, battling both mortal foes and supernatural entities in a tale driven by honor, revenge, and redemption. Blending elegant swordplay with stealth, spiritual abilities, and a dynamic day-night cycle, the game offers a cinematic experience enriched with hauntingly beautiful landscapes and deep narrative choices. Developed by Kojima Arts Studio, Ghost of Yōtei is poised to be one of 2025’s most anticipated titles, promising emotionally charged storytelling, refined combat, and a world where every shadow may conceal danger or destiny.

Key Features

  • > Hauntingly beautiful open world inspired by Hokkaido
  • > Real-time sword combat mixed with spirit abilities
  • > Deep lore with branching narrative choices
  • > Dynamic weather and day-night system
  • > Collect ancient relics and unlock spirit powers
